AI Excavator

The goal of this project was to train and evaluate an excavator bucket trajectory generation model to automatically dig terrain based on its geometric features. We used a Dynamic Movement Primitives (DMP) based system coupled with a Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP) as the shaping force modulator to generate the trajectory of the bucket.
I took part in the implementation of the model, and its training, evaluation and inference pipelines.

Topics : AI based heavy equipment, trajectory modeling.

Tech stack: Python, Pytorch.
