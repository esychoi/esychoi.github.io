The goal of this project was to train and evaluate an excavator bucket trajectory generation model, using geometric features of the terrain to dig.
We used a Dynamic Movement Primitives (DMP) based system coupled with a Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP) as the shaping force modulator to generate the trajectory of the bucket.
I took part in the implementation of the model, its training and evaluation.

Topics : AI based heavy equipment, trajectory modeling.
Tech stack: Python, Pytorch.
